Data Deletion Process

1. Right to Request Deletion

Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of personal data, users must contact the Company by sending an email to:

admin@norskporten.no

The request must be sent from the email address associated with the user’s account, or otherwise include sufficient information to verify the user’s identity.

3. Verification of Identity

For security and privacy reasons, the Company may require additional information to verify the identity of the requesting user before processing the deletion request.

4. Scope of Data Deletion

Upon successful verification, the Company will delete or anonymize all personal data associated with the user, including but not limited to:

  • Account information
  • User profile data
  • Usage data and learning progress
  • AI-generated content linked to the user account
  • Subscription and billing references (where legally permissible)

Data required to be retained for legal, accounting, or regulatory purposes may be stored for the minimum period required by law.

5. Deletion Timeline

All eligible personal data will be permanently deleted or anonymized within one (1) month from the date the deletion request is verified.

6. Exceptions and Legal Obligations

Certain data may be retained if required by applicable laws, including but not limited to:

  • Accounting and tax obligations
  • Fraud prevention
  • Compliance with legal or regulatory requirements

Such retained data will be securely stored and access will be strictly limited.

7. Effect of Deletion

Once the deletion process is completed:

  • The user account will be permanently deactivated
  • Access to the App and its services will be terminated
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered

8. Confirmation of Deletion

Upon completion of the deletion process, the Company may provide confirmation to the user via email.

9. Contact

For any questions regarding data deletion or privacy rights, users may contact: admin@norskporten.no
