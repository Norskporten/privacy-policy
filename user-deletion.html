Account Deletion

If you want to delete your account, we make the process simple and transparent.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to deletion@example.com from your registered email address with the subject line "Delete My Account".

What Gets Deleted

We remove your profile, settings, and any personal data tied to your account. Some records may be retained for legal or security purposes.

Timing

Deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation once the process is complete.

Need Help?

Contact support at support@example.com.
